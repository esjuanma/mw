District reimagined

Seaport Studios is a 5,000 square-foot two-story pop-up space that showcases a rotating selection of wares from emerging fashion designers. It also features an art gallery and a coffee bar. The project is a collaboration between Seaport owner Howard Hughes Corporation and Women's Wear Daily. It is envisioned as an eclectic mix of fashion, art, food and beverage in a curated environment.
