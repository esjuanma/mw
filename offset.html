Images like no other

Offset is a high end brand of an exceptional mix of photography and illustration; both commercial and editorial imagery sourced from broad categories including fashion, beauty, lifestyle, landscape, food, and still life. It features top assignment photographers from around the word who shoot for major advertising clients and name brands. The project was to add a blog section to the site. Going into designing it, it was crucial for me to keep the blog reflecting Offset distinctive brand identity: bold and striking.

— Role: Concept / Art Direction / Web Design
